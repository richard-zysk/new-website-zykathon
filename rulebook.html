🌟 ZyskaThon Rulebook

Hello, Zysk family! Get ready to dive into ZyskaThon – our very own coding fest. This is a time to bond, brainstorm, and build amazing things together.

🤝 Participation

Who’s In? All Zysk members. Because we believe in the power of unity.

👥 Team Formation

You're already grouped into teams. Thinking of a switch? Just chat with the event organizers.

🗓️ Mark the Calendar

It's coding time from 22nd September, 10:00 AM till 23rd September, 2:00 PM. Keep those dates free!

💡 The Challenge

Excited about what you'll be working on? Find the list of topics here. [hyperlink]
Remember, let’s innovate from scratch. So, stuff you've done before isn't part of this.

🧰 Toolkit

You’re free to pick your favorite tech tools. Unless we specifically ask you to stick to something, you’re in the driver's seat.

🏗️ Code Ownership

Whatever cool stuff we create during ZyskaThon stays right here with us at Zysk. And if you use someone else’s work, just mention them – it's the fair thing to do.

🤝 It’s All About Teamwork

Combine your strengths, work together, and create magic.
We’re all about keeping it in the family – so no outside help, please.
And a small reminder: Let’s be original and avoid copying.

🌟 Spotlight Moment

Once we’re done coding, each team will have 3 minutes to showcase their masterpiece. There's also a 2-minute slot for any questions.
We'll be checking out how creative your ideas are, how useful they could be, their design, and their impact.

❤️ Respect & Fun

At Zysk, we believe in having fun the right way. That means being nice, respecting everyone, and making sure everyone feels included.

🏆 The Reward

Top Spot: The best team walks away with INR 40,000 worth of prizes split among themselves.
Second Best: The next team isn't left behind – they get Amazon gift vouchers worth INR 10,000.
And the cherry on top? Winners get a special Zysk certificate and a big shout-out!

❓ Got Questions?

No worries! If you're ever in doubt or need some clarity, the Change Agents team is there to help.

📢 Staying Updated

Sometimes, things change. If we tweak these rules, you'll be the first to know.

❓Got Questions?:

No worries! If you're ever in doubt or need some clarity, the Tech Support team is there to help.

🎉 Lastly

ZyskaThon isn't just about coding. It's about coming together as a family, having fun, and leaving with awesome memories and learnings. Let’s make it a time to remember!
